Privacy Policy - Vijana Hub 255 1. Introduction Vijana Hub 255 is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our educational platform. We take the privacy of Tanzanian youth seriously and comply with applicable data protection laws in the United Republic of Tanzania. 2. Information We Collect 2.1 Personal Information We may collect the following personal information: Name and contact information (email address, phone number) Age or date of birth Educational level and school information Location (region/city in Tanzania) Account credentials (username and encrypted password) Profile information and preferences 2.2 Educational Data To provide personalized learning experiences: Learning progress and achievement records Quiz and assessment results Course enrollment and completion data Study patterns and preferences Content interactions and engagement metrics 2.3 Technical Information We automatically collect certain technical data: IP address and device information Browser type and version Operating system Usage statistics and analytics Cookies and similar tracking technologies 3. How We Use Your Information We use collected information for the following purposes: Providing and maintaining our educational services Personalizing learning experiences and content recommendations Tracking academic progress and generating performance reports Communicating with users about platform updates and educational content Improving our services and developing new features Ensuring platform security and preventing fraud Complying with legal obligations Conducting research to improve education in Tanzania 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure Vijana Hub 255 does not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances: 4.1 With Your Consent We may share information when you explicitly authorize us to do so. 4.2 Educational Institutions With your consent, we may share academic progress with your school or educational institution. 4.3 Parents and Guardians For users under 18, we may share information with verified parents or legal guardians. 4.4 Service Providers We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating Vijana Hub 255, subject to confidentiality agreements. 4.5 Legal Requirements We may disclose information when required by Tanzanian law or to protect our rights and safety. 5. Data Security We implement robust security measures to protect your information: Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest Secure server infrastructure with regular security audits Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only Regular security updates and patches Monitoring for suspicious activities and unauthorized access While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your login credentials confidential. 6. Children's Privacy Many of our users are minors. We take special care to protect children's privacy. For users under 18 years old: We require parental or guardian consent for account creation We collect only necessary information for educational purposes Parents can review, update, or delete their child's information We do not display personal information publicly without consent Community features are moderated to ensure child safety 7. Your Rights and Choices You have the following rights regarding your personal information: Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications Consent Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing To exercise these rights, contact us at info@vijanahub255.com 8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. Types of cookies we use: Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality Performance Cookies: Help us understand how you use our platform Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences Analytics Cookies: Measure and improve platform performance You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled. 9. Data Retention We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 90 days, unless we are required to retain it for legal purposes. 10. International Data Transfers Your information is primarily stored and processed in Tanzania. If we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this policy. 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of TanzaniaEdu after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. 12.
